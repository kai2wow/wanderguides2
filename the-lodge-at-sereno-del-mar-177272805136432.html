Welcome to The Lodge at Sereno Del Mar

Your serene coastal retreat awaits just four houses down from Portuguese Beach, where the sound of ocean waves and morning visits from neighboring ranch cows create the perfect backdrop for relaxation. Perched on half an acre backing up to a 600-acre ranch, this peaceful haven offers stunning views, easy beach access, and proximity to whale migration routes in spring. Whether you're here to unwind on the deck with your coffee or explore nearby trails, San Francisco, Russian River, and Napa, you've found the ideal base for both adventure and tranquility.

How to Use This Volunteer Guide

Follow these simple steps to find and participate in meaningful volunteer opportunities during your stay.

1

Browse Categories

Explore volunteer opportunities across five categories: Food, Animals, Community, Environment, and Creativity. Each organization has been vetted and welcomes short-term volunteers.

2

Check Schedules

Visit organization websites or call ahead to confirm volunteer hours and current needs. Many locations offer flexible drop-in opportunities perfect for travelers.

3

Sign Up

Reach out directly to your chosen organization to express interest. Some require brief orientations, while others welcome walk-in volunteers during specific hours.

4

Make Impact

Show up with an open heart and willingness to serve. Even a few hours of your time can make a significant difference in these local communities.

Pro Tip: Scroll down to explore our interactive map showing all volunteer locations pinned and ready for you to discover!
